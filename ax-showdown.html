How to Battle

Since searching for random Opponents doesn't work, you have to simply click a player and click on Challenge to battle.

Alternatively, you can use the command /challenge username, format where username is the player's username and format is the battle format (e.g., gen6axdoubles or gen6axsingles).

Battleformats and Ruleset

Current Battle Formats:

  • Doubles
  • Singles
  • Multi (Max Team Size = 3)

All formats follow a standard ruleset, which includes the removal of 'Swagger' and the 'Evasion Clause.'

We might introduce new formats in the Future!
